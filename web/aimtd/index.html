About AIMTD

AIMTD is an open-source toolkit for TD game development. The AIMTD API transforms your TD enemies from 'creeps' that mindlessly follow waypoints to intelligent monsters who choose their paths in real time.

Monster behavior patterns are put completely under your control - your monsters could follow the shortest path to the nearest objective, they could follow the path that's least likely to get them killed, or they could work together in teams to maximize their chances of reaching objectives. The API handles all the details of monster movement, allowing you to focus on other innovative game features. AIMTD is particularly well suited for TDs that use complicated maps, dynamic difficulty adjustments, adaptive multiplayer levels, or any other scenario that requires intelligent monster movement.

The API is written in Java. It currently supports waypoints, multiple map objectives, 2D monster movement in all 8 directions, and 3 distinct monster intelligence levels. Future versions of AIMTD will develop support for tower target locking, special monster attributes, and several other features.
