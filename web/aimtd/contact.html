Fact: AIMTD's awesomeness is awesome. Fact #2: the project's got a ton of room for growth and improvement. Here are just a few of AIMTD's planned features:

  • ActionScript integration
  • Tower intelligence
  • Dynamic difficulty adjustments
  • More advanced AI, like limited Monster field of vision
  • Support for extra game features, like Monsters' elemental resistances
  • ...and lots, lots more
The project's hope is to one day become a full-on framework for TD game development. If you want to get involved with the AIMTD project, or if you're developing a game using the AIMTD API, get in touch! You can reach me by email at fedenusy@gmail.com.
